Projects

Street workouts have become the epicenter of a revolution combininig calisthenics, prison workouts and bodyweight control. While at General Assembly London I decided to create a platform for this movement combining routines, forum and most importantly, an interactive Google Maps made for street athletes. The project was brought to life with a Ruby on Rails + Bootstrap + REST API stack.

After Urban Monkey I wanted to target another crowd, with another present-day problem. Why not developers? I decided to use the same stack, different database, MongoDB, and to focus on incorporating a cutting edge API: Stripe Payments. The finished product is a user-friendly portfolio builder for devs and creatives combined with a patronage/sponsoring platform. The result: Hacker Farm.
